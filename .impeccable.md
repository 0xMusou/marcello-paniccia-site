# Impeccable Design Context — Marcello Paniccia

## Design Context

### Users
Racing fans and media following the Mazda MX-5 Cup series. They arrive curious — they want to feel the driver's energy, check results, and stay connected to the season. They're not evaluating a resume; they're experiencing a story. The experience should feel like flipping through a motorsport editorial, not browsing a stats page.

### Brand Personality
**Intense · Fast · Authentic**

Marcello is a young Canadian driver with real hunger — nothing manufactured. The brand should feel like the sport itself: high-stakes, visceral, and honest. Not polished for a boardroom. Polished for the grid.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Dark-first**: #0a0a0a background, everything layered against near-black
- **Red as energy**: #C32323 is the heartbeat — used sparingly but with intention (key values, accents, CTAs)
- **Green as contrast**: #1E9156 for secondary moments — off-track, lifestyle, balance
- **Type as design**: Gambarino (display serif, 400) for headlines — editorial weight, not athletic grotesque. Switzer for body — clean, neutral, gets out of the way.
- **Photos do the work**: Full-bleed, high-contrast imagery. Gradients serve the photo, not the other way around.
- **Anti-reference**: No generic sports templates — no stock-photo heroes, no blue gradients, no numbered stat grids with stock icons. Every layout decision should feel intentional and editorial.

### Design Principles
1. **Restraint amplifies impact** — Use red once and it hits hard. Use it everywhere and it disappears. Same rule applies to every design element: type size, spacing, animation, contrast.
2. **Speed without gimmick** — Animations should feel like the car moving, not a demo reel. GSAP entrances, Lenis scroll, parallax — all in service of momentum, never for show.
3. **Photography is the hero** — Layouts exist to frame the photos. When in doubt, give the image more room. Gradients, overlays, and type placement should enhance what's already there.
4. **Editorial over athletic** — Think motorsport magazine spread, not team website. Asymmetric layouts, staggered type, varied photo shapes. The unexpected detail is what makes it feel real.
5. **Authentic over perfect** — The site should feel like it has a point of view. A personality. Marcello is 21, Canadian, fighting for every position — the design should carry that weight, not sanitize it.

### Tech Constraints
- Vanilla HTML/CSS/JS — no framework
- GSAP 3 + ScrollTrigger for animation
- Lenis for smooth scroll
- Gambarino (OTF local), Switzer (OTF local)
- Targeting Webflow migration — keep structure clean and transferable
- Accessibility: basic (keyboard nav + alt text, no WCAG compliance required)
